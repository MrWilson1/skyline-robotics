Our Appreciation

We at Spartabots are tremendously thankful for the support and help of our sponsors, mentors, parents, and other volunteers. Without them, we can honestly say that our Robotics club would never be able to function.

By either donating tools, money, time, or their knowledge, our sponsors, mentors, and volunteers made it possible for our robotics club to both function and grow.

To show our appreciation for them, we've decided to create an entire section of our website devoted to thanking them.

Our Sponsors

Without the money that our sponsors provide, we wouldn't be able to purchase parts for our robots, or attend competitions. Learn more about our sponsors.

Our Mentors

Our mentors provide valuable help and advice to our team. We're extremely grateful how they spend so much time helping and teaching members of our team. Learn more about our mentors.

Our Parents and other Volunteers

From buying dinner, to driving members of our team around, we're grateful for all of their assistance to the team. Learn more about our parents and other volunteers here.
