Who are we?

We are Spartabots, a FIRST robotics team based in Skyline High School in Sammamish, Washington. Founded in 2008, our team is distinguished by our members' fervent commitment to fulfilling FIRST's mission of inspiring pursuit and awareness of science and technology throughout our community. Over the next few years, our growing team is eager to expand our outreach by mentoring local teams, educating new and potential members, and providing opportunities for the public to interact with our robots.

Our Mission Statement

We, the Spartabots, are committed to furthering the student and public knowledge in programming, tools, and other skills associated with the development of a functional robot in the interest of promoting the future scientists and leaders of the STEM (Science Technology Engineering and Math) fields.
