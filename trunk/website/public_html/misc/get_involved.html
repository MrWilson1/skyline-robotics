Getting Involved

Join the club!

Robotics club will accept members during any point of the year. Of course, you have to be an ASB member at Skyline High School, and meet ASB's requirements for club membership, but apart from that, there are no restrictions. Contact us.

Mentoring

If you have experience in engineering or programming, or even in public relations or web design, we could certainly use your help! Adult mentoring is one of the key parts of FIRST's philosophy, and it's not uncommon to encounter teams with over 20 mentors. Contact us.

Donating

We're always in need of money for donations! Between having to buy parts for robots, and having to buy tools to build them, we're always short-charged on cash.

Unfortunately, we currently do not have an easy way set up for donations yet. If you desperately want to donate, Contact us online or talk to one of our club members.
