Our Mentors

We would like to thank the following mentors for their invaluable time and support in inspiring and enabling us to pursue FIRST's mission, and helping coordinating the development and construction of our robots.

Blair Cooper

Mr. Cooper has mentored our team since its inception, generously providing our team with a workspace, access to tools, and his knowledge in regards to the design, circuitry, programming and building of our robots. He currently works for Research in Motion.

Hearen Shim

Mrs. Shim is the advisor for our club and is a perpetual source of guidence and advice. She teaches math at our school, Skyline High School.

Kiet Nguyen

Mr. Nguyen mentors our team in building and design, as well as facilitating the purchase and transportation of our materials. He is currently an engineer at Boeing.

Kim Covey

Mrs. Covey is an engineer at Boeing, and has aided our team through her knowledge of design and the organization and management of our team.

Robert Covey

Mr. Covey is an engineer at Boeing, offering our team his expertise in design and building, along with helping us through the transportaion and purchase of our materials.
