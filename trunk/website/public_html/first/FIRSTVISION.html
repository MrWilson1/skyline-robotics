FIRST's Vision and Mission

"To transform our culture by creating a world where science and technology are celebrated and where young people dream of becoming science and technology leaders."

- Dean Kamen, Founder

Today, the world revolves around the STEM feilds. The world relies on leaders in those fields to innovate and evolve our world. Dean Kamen is focusing not on personal gain, but reaching out to everyone to ingrain the seeds of innovation. He wants the youth to gain interest in leading brilliant new ideas in the STEM fields to give for the benefit of our world.

FIRST's mission is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.

FIRST, although victory is important, one must win for the sake of competition, not for the sake of feeling good. FIRST's goal is to simply raise awareness through competition so that the passion exerted in competition will reflect in the leadership a FIRST member will take in the steps to insprie and innovate. The foundation of FIRST is to motivate the youth to greatness.
