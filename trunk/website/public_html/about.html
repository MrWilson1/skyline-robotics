About Us

We are Spartabots, a robotics team based in Skyline High School in Sammamish, Washington. Each year, we participate in a robotics competition hosted by FIRST, an organization dedicated to robotics.

Founded in 2008, out team is distingished by our members' fervent commitment to fulfilling FIRST's mission of inspiring pursuit and awareness of science and technology within our school and community. Over the next few years, our growing team is eager to expand our outreach by mentoring localc teams, educating new and potential members, and providng opportunities for the public to interact and learn about robotics and science.
