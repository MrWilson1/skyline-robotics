What we do

Note: Names and photographs are being withheld until permission forms have been turned in.

Build Team

The build team is responsible for building the robot.

Design

The design team is responsible for creating schematics of the robot using CAD. By first 'virtually creating' the robot, we can get much of the prototyping done virtually first, allowing us to construct our robot more efficently.

Programming

The programming team programs the robot while it is being build. Our team programs in C++.

Media

The media team works with the other teams to document and photograph our progress. They work together with our public relations team to provide pictures and stories to raise awareness of our club.

Public Relations

The public relations team is responsible primarily for raising awareness of our club. They are also responsible for starting fundraisers.

Web Design

The web design team is responsible for maintaining our website. They work together with the media team and the public relations team to help establish a solid presence on the internet. See more infomation about our website.

Mentors

Our mentors are a group of adult volunteers that help teach and guide our club in various aspects, such as build, design, fundraising, and leadership. See why they're awesome here.
