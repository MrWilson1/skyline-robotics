Meeting this Thursday

2011 May 02

We'll be having another meeting this coming Thursday (May 5th) right after school, in Mrs. Shim's room.

The agenda for the meeting:

  • Finalize details about the picnic (time and date, what food to bring, etc.
  • Finalize details about the farmer's market (time and date, making flyers, etc.)
  • Discuss how and when to revive the robots.
  • Continue discussing the possibility of being mentored (in programming and/or CAD) during the summer?
  • Discuss if we should try recruiting middle-schoolers?

(If I'm missing anything, or if you think something should be changed, let me know.)

We've already discussed some of these topics over email, but I think it's important to bring them up formally in a meeting so that there's no confusion about (for example) who's responsible for bringing plates, or something or other...

Also, our website is looking a little empty: if you have pictures or videos of us during the build sessions, during the competitions, or anything related to robotics, and feel comfortable sharing, please send them in to me or anybody on the website team! We'll put them on the website, and make a media gallery that we can show off to people.
