Meeting Summary

2011 April 28

Meeting agenda:

  • Should we set up a booth at the Farmer's Market?
  • How can we revive our robots so we can demo them?
  • Where should we have our end-of-year picnic?
  • Presenting general website info

Resolved:

  • We will be setting up a booth at the Farmer's Market.
  • We will contact Mr. Cooper on more info on how to revive the two robots.
  • We will be going to Pine Lake for our picnic

Unresolved:

  • The logistics of manning a booth at the Farmer's Market.
  • The date and other details of the end-of-year picnic

Additional Info:

We delayed discussing logistics for the Farmer's Market and the end-of-year picnic because we had a very low attendence today. Hopefully, we will be discussing these issues in the next meeting.

The website is slowly getting off the ground, and the main template and theme has been finished. However, the website currently lacks both content and graphics, so we've started assigning those tasks to various people. If you have any pictures or videos to share, want to contribute code or content (or anything else), or want to be assigned a specific task, email me at deflect.4444@gmail.com

We will start adding lots of fancier features when we finally have ASB's approval to purchase our domain and set up an official website, such as forums, Wordpress, RSS feeds, etc... But for now, our focus is currently on content.

We don't have comments yet.
