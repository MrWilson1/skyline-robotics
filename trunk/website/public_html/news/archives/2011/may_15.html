Meeting Summary

2011 May 11

Hi everyone, in case you missed today's meeting, here is a quick overview of the topics we discussed.

End of Year Party:

As previously mentioned, May 21st will not work as people have various other commitments (i.e. Relay for Life) on that day. The only other date available at Pine Lake Park before the school year ends which is on a Saturday is May 28th. Please reply to this email if you have a problem with this date. As May 28th is on Memorial Day Weekend, people may be going out of town and we need to know how many people can actually attend. Also, include your opinion about a party on a Sunday or a suggestion for a certain weekday.

The sign up sheet for food is still going around so please email Sampurna at pifor23@gmail.com with any contributions you are willing to make.

The Robot:

Our Robotics equipment has made it to Skyline! A big thanks to Dom who picked it up from Mr. Cooper's house.

Next week, we will start to revive the robot in order to get it ready for future demonstrations.

Website:

Website Team: Keep a look-out for announcement emails from Michael.

Farmer's Market:

Our Farmer's Market booth will be on Wednesday, June 29th. Michael and Lalith are currently the full-time volunteers at our booth; if you would like to help out, please email Amy.

We need informational fliers and a poster for our booth. If someone would like to start working on content and design, please feel free to do so and bring your ideas to the next meeting.

Mentoring:

Mr. Kumaressen's colleague is available Mondays, Wednesdays, and weekends after 5 PM (this is including the summer). Further information for those interested in honing their programming skills will be available at the next meeting.

Posters for Next Year:

In order to streamline the ASB approval process for next year, we have decided to start working on poster designs now. If you have ideas, please feel free to start working on them and bring them to the next meeting.

Hope to see you all at the next meeting! (Thursday May 19th).
